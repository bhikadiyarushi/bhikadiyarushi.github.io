WHY PHILIPPINES ?

⮕ The Philippines, officially the Republic of the Philippines, is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia.

⮕ Philippines offer quality MBBS education with economical tuition fees.

⮕ If we compare with other US countries then tuition fees is quiet low and education pattern is similar to US study based pattern.

⮕ In Philippines, MBBS is called as MD (Doctor of Medicine).

⮕ MBBS from Philippines is getting popular for MBBS aspirants from across the world because MBBS is taught in English.

⮕ Unlike China or Russia, medical students don't need to learn an additional language to study MBBS courses.

⮕ There are various universities in Philippines which are recognized by MCI like Perpetual Help, Bicol Medical College, Angeles Medical university, etc.

⮕ There are several low cost medical colleges for Indian students in Philippines.
